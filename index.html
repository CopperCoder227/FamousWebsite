✍ Leonardo di ser Piero da Vinci 🎨

Leonardo Di Vinci (Full name : Leonardo di ser Piero da Vinci) was a famous artist and creationist. He made many revolutionary items for his time, such as the mona lisa, and a book written entirely backwards so people couldn't decipher his secret thoughts and feelings, or so he thought! this website will be a helpful guide to Leonardo, and the astonishing life this man once lived. Born Leonardo di ser Piero da Vinci · 15 April 1452 · (possibly Anchiano), Vinci, Republic of Florence Died 2 May 1519 (aged 67) · Clos Lucé, Amboise, Kingdom of France Education Studio of Andrea del Verrocchio
